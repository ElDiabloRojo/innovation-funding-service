This is an IFS component.

Examples

Red and green alerts have matching colour text. All other alerts have black text.

Blue info alert

To make the user aware of something and prompt them to act. One link can be within the text. More than one link should be in a bulleted list.

You must complete your project and bank details within 30 days of our notification to you.

All project partners have individual steps to complete in this section.

View the status of partners

Green complete alert

To show an action is complete.

Application details are marked as complete

Grey pending alert

To provide visual feedback that action is waiting on someone else. e.g. another partner or Innovate UK.

The bank details you submitted are being reviewed by Innovate UK.

Red alert

To draw attention to something that is blocked and requires immediate action to proceed.

You have a pending finance query to respond to.

Black assigned alert

To inform the user of who this question is assigned to.

Assigned to you.

contact your Innovate UK monitoring officer to discuss a Project Change Request

We may be able to provide you with monthly funding to accelerate the grant towards your eligible project costs rather than the standard quarterly reimbursement. You will still have to fund your costs that are not covered by the grant.

We may be able to provide you with an Innovation Continuity Loan of between £250,000 and £1,600,000 for a period of up to 7 years to support your project costs that are not covered by your grant. You will have to pay interest on the amount that you borrow and you will have to repay the loan. You will also have to provide us with financial information so that we can assess the continued financial viability of your business. Link to Innovation Continuity Loan application.

We may be able to provide you with a Continuity Grant up to £250,000 to support your project and reasonable business costs that are not covered by your grant. [Link to continuity grant application].
